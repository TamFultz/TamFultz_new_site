Linked Data Bibliography

Linked Data Introductions (list from Melanie Wacker)

  • Introducing Linked Data and The Semantic Web (LinkedDataTools.com) Tutorial including RDF, RDFSs, and OWL
  • LD4PE–Explore Linked Data Learning Resource
  • Linked Data – Connect Distributed Data across the Web (Tom Heath) Free e-books, tutorials, and presentations
  • BIBFRAME Training at Library of Congress
  • EUCLID–Educational Curriculum for the usage of Linked Data
  • Report on Available Linked Data Training Resources (PCC Standing Committee on Training)

Monographs

  • Brath, Richard, and David Jonker. Graph Analysis and Visualization: Discovering Business Opportunity in Linked Data. 1 edition. Wiley, 2015.
  • Godby, Carol Jean, Shenghui Wang, and Jeffrey K. Mixter. Library Linked Data in the Cloud: OCLC’s Experiments with New Models of Resource Description. Morgan & Claypool Publishers, 2015.
  • Hart, Glen, and Catherine Dolbear. Linked Data: A Geographic Perspective. CRC Press, 2016.
  • Harth, Andreas, Katja Hose, and Ralf Schenkel, eds. Linked Data Management. 1 edition. Chapman and Hall/CRC, 2016.
  • Heath, Tom, and Christian Bizer. Linked Data: Evolving the Web into a Global Data Space. Morgan & Claypool Publishers, 2011.
  • Hooland, Seth van, and Ruben Verborgh. Linked Data for Libraries, Archives and Museums: How to Clean, Link and Publish Your Metadata. Amer Library Assn Editions, 2014.
  • Jones, Ed, and Michele Seikel. Linked Data for Cultural Heritage. Chicago: Amer Library Assn Editions, 2016.
  • Sikos, Ph D., Leslie F. Mastering Structured Data on the Semantic Web: From HTML5 Microdata to Linked Open Data. 1 edition. Apress, 2015.
  • Wood, David, Marsha Zaidman, Luke Ruth, and Michael Hausenblas. Linked Data: Structured Data on the Web. 1 edition. Shelter Island, NY: Manning Publications, 2014.
