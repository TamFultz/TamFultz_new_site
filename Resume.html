Professional Experience

THE METROPOLITAN MUSEUM OF ART

June 2005-present

Associate Museum Librarian, (Cataloger/Reference Librarian) – performs original and complex cataloging chiefly of special collections material on Innovative Millennium and OCLC Connexion Client. Creates metadata for books as objects in the museums content management system, TMS. Maintains awareness of changes in national resource description standards and helps formulate local policies. Performs authority work according to NACO and SACO standards as a member of the authorities’ team. Provides reference service at Watson and Nolen library reference desks. Coordinates the training of staff in complex cataloging procedures as team leader to the cataloging instruction team. Selects and orders Greek language and classical art & archaeology materials. Was part of a two-member team that trained staff in RDA guidelines and procedures.

June 2000-June 2005

Assistant Museum Librarian (Cataloger/Reference Librarian) – performed original and copy cataloging on RLIN21. Performed local authority work. Participated in bibliographic database maintenance. Assisted patrons at reference desk in use of print and online resources. Served on library projects and search committees as needed. Served as the chair of the search committee for the position: Manager of Bibliographic Operations. Served on the off-site storage committee.

THE AMERICAN NUMISMATIC SOCIETY

1997-2000

Assistant Librarian/Cataloger - performed original cataloging on MITINET in MARC format of: monographs, pamphlets, offprints, journal articles, serials, rare books, archival materials, video-recordings, and other materials. Updated and maintained online authority file. Oversaw retrospective conversion project in regards to adherence to AACR2R principles and USMARC format. Assisted patrons and supervised part time staff in absence of Head Librarian.
